⚔️ The Teutonic Order Calls for Odin ⚔️

Dear Odin, Knight of Valor

The fires in the great hall have dimmed without your tales.
Your shield still hangs in the armory — polished, waiting.

The battles rage on, but your absence is felt more deeply than any wound.

You were not merely a warrior — you were our compass, our thunder, our kin. The Order remains, but it is not whole. A blade without its edge. A banner without its wind.

We write not to summon — but to remind you of who you are.
You are Odin, and you are ours.

Made with honor by your brothers of the Teutonic Order.
