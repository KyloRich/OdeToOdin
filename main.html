The Hochmeister's Call

To Knight-Lord Odin,

In our hour of most dire need, we call out to you, our brother. We ask you once more to raise your shield in defense of the Order.

For the country and our people's livelihoods are at stake. The Teutonic Knights have once more gathered to hoist the white and black upon their chest.

Let it be known that the Order is not now and not ever fully whole without your courage. Your sacrifice, bravery, and unwillingness for compromise is what makes you our most remarkable soldier.

For who do we look toward for joy and light in our darkest hours? During the most crushing defeats, you are there — a voice of reason and reinvigoration of the spirit.

To once more march against our foes, we call out to you not just as a soldier, but as a brother of this holy Order.

Let it be known that we will always have a seat for you at our table, and your companionship will always be sorely missed.

Your brother always,
Hochmeister Belisarius

Etched in steel. Bound by fire. Remembered always.
