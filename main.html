⚔️ The Teutonic Order Calls for Odin ⚔️
"He who rides with the Order never rides alone."

To Our Brother, Odin

The hall stands colder. The mead goes untouched. The fire whispers your name.

Your banner, black and white, still hangs above the stone. Your brothers still carry your stories into battle. But no tale compares to your return.

The Order endures, but it is quieter. Like a war drum without rhythm. Like steel without a wielder.

We do not beg. We do not plead. We simply remind you:
You are Odin. And the Teutonic Order is not complete without you.

Raised by your brethren. For honor. For Odin. For the Order.
